My Work

Present Position(09/2015-)

I am a Post Doctoral Research Fellow working with Biplab Sanyal at Department of Physics and Astronomy, Materials Theory, Uppsala University.

Previous Position(07/12-08/15)

Worked as Research Associate with Julie Staunton at Department of Physics, University of Warwick.

Research Interest

My research interest is electronic and magnetic properties of disordered materials.

I am currently working on magnetocaloric effect and magnetic anisotropy in disordered materials. I use first-princiles based on Density functional Theory to study the magnetic properties of system at finite temperature.
